How to play

Beat your opponent by being the first to get to a final score of 100.

  1. Click on the Roll button to roll the dice. Each roll of the dice will be added to your current score.
  2. Rolling a one ,on the dice, will reset your current score to 0 and switch to the next player.
  3. To avoid loosing your current score, click the Hold button to save your score.
  4. Each time the hold button is clicked, your current score will be added to your total score. The first player to get a total score of 100 is the winner.
